Why study Computer Science?

Get a degree in computer science and set out on an adventure full of creativity and opportunities. Learn the art of problem-solving, and help to influence our digital world's future. Start exploring the world of creativity, coding, and limitless chances right now !!!.

Undergraduate Courses

Our undergraduate programs are geared to give students the information, abilities, and practical experience they need to succeed in the fast-paced world of technology. Some of the programmes we offer are; Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) and Information Technology.

Explore advanced studies in Computer Science

Improve your skills with our prestigious graduate programs at University of Ghana Computer Science Department. Our MPhil, MSc, and PhD programs meet your goals whether they involve extensive research, thorough coursework, or doctorate studies that have the potential to change the world.
